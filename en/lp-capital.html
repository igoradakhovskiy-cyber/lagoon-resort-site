FAQ

Legal and investment questions

Will the project be completed? Who is the developer?
The project is led by a group with hotel operator HSC Group Hospitality; the flagship hotel within the resort operates under the Hilton brand. The financial model has been verified by Cushman & Wakefield. Construction progress is shared openly for every building: site photos and drone footage with dates, plus a live webcam.
How is my money protected?
The transaction is formalized with an official purchase-and-sale agreement, payments follow a schedule during construction, and your title is registered with Georgia's National Agency of Public Registry (NAPR). Foreigners own property 100%.
Are returns of up to 16% a year realistic?
Returns are calculated in the Cushman & Wakefield financial model using real Batumi market data: ADR, occupancy and price growth. The model accounts for the 60% owner's share, the service fee and taxes. Full quarter-by-quarter tables for each apartment type are in the presentation.
Can foreigners own property in Georgia?
Yes, foreign nationals own residential property 100% without restrictions. Title registration takes 1 day through NAPR, with a state fee from $55.
What taxes do I pay as an investor?
0% on purchase, 0% capital-gains tax (after 2 years of ownership), 0% property tax in most cases and 0% on rental income (income flows through the management company). Consultation with a tax specialist is recommended before operation begins.
What does management cover, and how do I receive income?
The management company handles occupancy, pricing, guest rotation, maintenance and taxes. All units join one shared revenue pool — each investor earns the same income per unit regardless of floor or building. The owner's share is 60% of revenue.
How do the installments work?
A $10,000 down payment at booking. Then interest-free installments of $850 a month for up to 30 months, with the balance due at handover. Mortgage financing is also available.
When is handover and the start of income?
Rental operation starts in 2028. The model spans 10 operating years, including a later resale of the asset, whose value grows +50–57% by 2037.
